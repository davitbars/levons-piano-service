Meet Levon Terteryan

My name is Levon Terteryan, and I was born in Armenia into a family rich in music heritage. My father was a classical singer, music teacher, and choir conductor. I studied at the Erevan Komitas Conservatory and graduated in 1982 with a Master’s degree in Violin Performance.

In 1989 we performed at Carnegie Hall with the State Academic Opera and Ballet Symphony Orchestra. Additionally, we have performed in Los Angeles, San Francisco, Boston, Wiesbaden Germany,and many more world renowned theatres. In 1991, I moved with my family to the United States.

My passion for music evolved into a career in piano servicing and restoration. Since founding Levon’s Piano Service in 1994, I’ve had the privilege of restoring, tuning, voicing, and regulating some of the finest pianos in the world. In 1996, I contracted with European Piano Craft in Long Beach, where the uncrompomised craft of classic piano restoration is skillfully preserved.
